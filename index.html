TREES FOR CITIES

We improve lives by planting trees in cities

Trees for Cities is a UK charity which aims to plant urban trees and create greener cities.
Since 1993, the organisation has reported that 125,000 volunteers have planted
over 1,200,000 urban trees in parks, streets, woodlands, schools, hospitals and housing estates.
We get stuck in with local communities to cultivate lasting change in their neighbourhoods.

We are looking for volunteers!
